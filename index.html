NANDA

Networked Agents and Decentralized AI

An open research initiative developing infrastructure for the Agentic Web. Building decentralized systems that enable autonomous AI agents to collaborate without centralized control.

About

NANDA is a research project focused on creating decentralized infrastructure for AI agent collaboration. We develop open source tools and protocols that enable autonomous agents to discover, communicate, and coordinate with each other across distributed networks.
